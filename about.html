About

Hi, I'm Mike. I have problems:

  • It takes time to re-find recipes that I have previously followed that are on the internet somehwere.
  • It takes time to read through recipe posts and glean the important steps from the onslaught of tangential comments and advertisements.

This blog is my solution - My own cookbook that is easy to find and easy to read.

If you are reading this and have these problems too, feel free to check out my recipes!
